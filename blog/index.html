üçÆ Recipe - Caramel Egg Custard

Created: 20 April 2024, Saturday


Make the caramel


Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup of sugar
  2. 1/4 cup of water

  • Heat the sugar and water in a small pot
  • In medium heat, stir and melt sugar to dissolve the sugar
  • Once syrup boils, reduce to low heat and let it simmer
  • Once it starts to turn golden brown, turn off the heat, as the caramel could burn quickly
  • If the sugar turns into crystals, you can add more water and just start over

Make the custard


Ingredients:

  1. 4 cups of milk
  2. 1/2 cup of sugar
  3. 6 eggs
  4. some vanilla essence

  • Mix the milk and sugar over low heat - to warm milk and to melt sugar
  • Mix eggs and vanilla into the milk
  • Strain the egg mixture 2 times - to have smooth texture

Put it all together


Things to have:

  1. Individual Ramekins or baking tray(s) - to put your custard
  2. Extra larger size baking tray - put some hot water

  • Pour the caramel into the ramekin / baking tray
  • Swirl caramel around until it covers the bottom of the tray
  • Pour in the egg mixture
  • Put the custard-try into the hot-water-tray
  • Put it in a pre-heated oven (180¬∞C)
  • Bake until the custard sets (around 30-45 minutes)
  • You can eat it hot or cold
