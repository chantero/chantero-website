  • Tenancy Ad-valorem Stamp Duty Formula:

    [Monthly Rent] x 12 months - RM2,000 / 250 = N

    * 1 year term: N
    * 1 year to 3 years term: N x 2
    * 3 Years term and above: N x 4

    [Additional copies will be RM10 each]

  • Sabah Tenancy Legal Fees Formula:

    ([Monthly Rent] x 12 months) - 12,000) * 1.75 / 100 + 700
