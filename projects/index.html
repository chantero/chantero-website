Calculators


  • Tenancy Calculator - Calculate estimated ad-valorem stamp duties and legal fees for tenancy agreements in Sabah, Malaysia.

  • Reverse Calculator - Calculate the original "fees excluding tax" based on [total fees including tax] and the [tax rate].

  • Date Calculator - Calculate the number of days between 2 dates.

  • Compound Interest Calculator - A very simple compound interest calculator to calculate amount based on [principal sum], [interest rate] and [years].

  • Acres Roods Perches Calculator - Calculate Acres Roods and Perches into Acres, Square Feet, Hectares or Square Meters.


Mini Tools


  • Number to Ringgit - Convert a number into a Ringgit Malaysia representation of the number.

  • Case Changer - Change the "case" of a text to uppercase, title case, or lowercase.

  • Dash Remover - Use this to remove all "dashes" from text. The purpose of this tool is so that I can easily paste/copy "dashless" NRIC numbers.

  • CSV Copyboard - Generate a "copyboard" based on a CSV file.
